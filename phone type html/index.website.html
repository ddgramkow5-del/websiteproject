IAbout Our College

Rooted in Tradition, Growing in Purpose

Our History

Founded through the shared vision of two educational charisms, the college began as a small parish school and has since grown into a full learning community serving thousands of students across all levels.

Each decade added new chapters — new buildings, new programs, and new generations of graduates carrying forward the same founding spirit of faith and formation.

  • 1960s — FoundationThe college opens its doors to its first cohort of students.
  • 1980s — ExpansionNew academic buildings and programs are established.
  • 2000s — GrowthEnrollment surpasses several thousand students.
  • Today — RenewalA modern campus continuing a decades-long mission.

VVision

A learning community recognized for forming competent, faith-filled, and service-oriented individuals who lead with integrity.

MMission

To provide accessible, quality, values-based education that develops the whole person — mind, heart, and character — for life and service.
